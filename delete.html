How to Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your Ferne account and all associated data by following the steps below. Once confirmed, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

  1. Option 1 — In the App Open Ferne → Go to your Profile tab → Tap Edit Profile → Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete My Account".
  2. Option 2 — By Email Send an email to ferne.app@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your Ferne account.
⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your health data, logs, and insights will be removed.

What Gets Deleted

Data Type Action Retention
Account & email address Deleted Within 30 days
Health logs (hot flashes, symptoms, mood, sleep, period) Deleted Within 30 days

Profile information (name, date of birth, phase) Deleted Within 30 days
